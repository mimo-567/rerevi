# Eduqas GCSE RS — Spec Map (Route A: Christianity + Islam)

> The content backbone. Every quote, keyword, note, and question links to a
> **spec-point ID** from this map. Built from the official
> [Eduqas GCSE RS specification (from 2016)](https://www.eduqas.co.uk/media/w42hvhgp/eduqas-gcse-rs-spec-full-from-2016-e-1109.pdf).
>
> **How to read this:** each theme/section lists its **Areas of Study** (= spec
> points, with IDs), the **Concepts** (→ become keyword decks), and the
> **scripture/sources** the spec explicitly cites (kept as **reference context** for
> keywords/questions/notes — the quote bank is no longer built; see scope note).
> Spec-point IDs reuse the theme tag from [`content-model.md`](./content-model.md).

## Assessment structure

| Component | Title | Exam | Weight |
|-----------|-------|------|--------|
| 1 | Religious, Philosophical & Ethical Studies (4 themes) | 2 hours | 50% |
| 2 | Study of Christianity (beliefs + practices) | 1 hour, 60 marks | 25% |
| 3 | Study of a World Faith — **Islam** (beliefs + practices) | 1 hour, 60 marks | 25% |

In Component 1 each theme is studied from a **Christian** and a **non-religious**
perspective and one other religion — for this site that other religion is
**Islam**. So Component 1 quotes/notes carry Christian (`C`), Muslim (`M`), and
non-religious (`P`) views.

## Component 1 — Themes (50%)

### Theme 1 · Relationships — tag `RLP`
| ID | Area of study | Covers |
|----|---------------|--------|
| `RLP-1` | Relationships | Nature & purpose of relationships, families, roles of men & women, marriage (incl. outside the faith), cohabitation, adultery, divorce, annulment, separation, re-marriage |

## How this map drives the build

- **Spec points** (`RLP-1`, `CB-3`, `IP-1`, …) are the join key across all content.
- **Concepts** become **keyword decks** (one per theme/component).
- **Coverage tracking** (the later RAG feature) checks off against these IDs.

> **Scope note:** a **quote bank is no longer built** (`re.ibzz.uk` covers quotes).
> The **seed sources** listed per theme above are kept as **reference context** for
> writing keywords, questions, and (later) notes — not as a quote collection. The
> source-based quote-ID scheme in `content-model.md` is parked.
